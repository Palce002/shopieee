About HABI

HABI is more than just streetwear – it's a statement. Born from the rebellious spirit of young women who refuse to be silenced, our brand embodies the raw energy and unapologetic attitude of today's generation.

Inspired by the fearless authenticity of artists like Olivia Rodrigo, HABI creates pieces that speak to the bold, the brave, and the beautifully chaotic. Every design tells a story of empowerment, self-expression, and the courage to be different.

Our Creators

Founded by a collective of young designers and artists who believe fashion should be a form of rebellion, HABI continues to push boundaries and challenge conventions in the streetwear space.
